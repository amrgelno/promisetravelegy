* Cruise the waters of the Nile river.
* Sailing the River Nile on Nile Voyager felucca and Discover Egypt’s ancient past visiting temples and historical sites along the River Nile.
* The guests Of Nile Voyager will have the privilege to visit exceptionally unusual monuments such as El Kab and Silsilla, to discover the temples there; you will fully feel the magic of these great places…
* Enjoy riverside dining on Nile Voyager whilst viewing the beautiful surroundings.

Nile Voyager is a newly-built felucca, the traditional sailing boat of Egypt, measuring 11.5 meters by 4.5 meters.
She was designed and constructed in Esna by Sultan, a well-known ship-builder of the Nile Valley.
